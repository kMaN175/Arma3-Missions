To pass the training/course a shooter must have a passing score of 180 out of a possible 220.  To acheive the minimum passing score, a shooter needs to score the following;

KD Range:
minimum should be 6 hits out of 10 = 60 points

Field-Fire Range:
minimum should be 7 hits out of 12 = 70 points

CS Range:
shooter must kill the enemy smiper = 50 points
